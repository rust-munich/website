How We Meet

We host one event per quarter — alternating between talks and hands-on hacking evenings.

Talks

Q1 & Q3 (odd quarters)

Community members and guests share what they've built, learned, or discovered. Typically 2-3 talks per evening, followed by discussion and networking.

Hacking Evenings

Q2 & Q4 (even quarters)

Small groups (<25 people) working together on a shared challenge. We pick a project, hack on it, and learn from each other.
